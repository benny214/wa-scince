Science Intensive Software Development

At WaveAccess, we develop custom software that drives scientific research & development processes.

Our in-house team has engineers with MSc and PhD degrees in the relevant fields (e.g. computer science, computational physics, materials science, genetics), which enables us to maintain lasting partnerships with science-intensive companies, such as Thomson Reuters.

Check out a new case about the creation of an information system for processing large arrays of genetic data.

Download the Case now

Many scientific research companies either have driven, or will, through process reengineering as they try to streamline operations and increase efficiency. At the heart of these process reengineering efforts are new information systems.

Information system development is an opportunity to restructure traditional work methods by creating new processes between employees, their computer tools and their professional operating tasks. The knowledge that is necessary for redesigning traditional work practices cannot be acquired by means of consultations, interviews, observations or other forms of analysis. Instead, it must be constructed in a process involving all parties interested in system development.

So, it is essential that the information system is built by a team that includes scientists who will be using it and developers capable of speaking the same language with them.
